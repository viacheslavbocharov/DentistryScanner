Treatment

Crowns are suitable for people who need a partial or full smile makeover procedure with major teeth defects such as decayed teeth, chipped or misshapen teeth, discolouration or disproportionately large spaces between teeth.

Timeline: one 5-day trip

Day 1
Consultation
The dentist will examine your teeth, take an X-ray and discuss your dental history with medical contraindications. Next, the dentist will confirm the possibility of installing crowns and start the treatment with your consent.
1
Day 1
Smile Design
The dentist will discuss your wishes and advise on the shape of the crowns to achieve an aesthetic smile based on the anatomy of your face. The dentist will give recommendations on the choice of translucency, brightness and colour of the crowns.
2
Day 2
Preparation
The dentist will remove a thin layer of surface enamel all around using local anaesthetics. Next, the dentist will make a mould in order to construct the crowns. After that, you will be provided with temporary crowns before the permanent ones are placed.
3
Day 5
Placing
The dentist polishes the tooth and then etches it. After that, dental cement is applied to the tooth and a crown is attached. With the help of focused light, the cement is activated and the crown is fixed on the tooth.
4

Dental Trip Process

1
Consultation
Send us your X-ray (if available) + photos of your teeth via our "Free Consultation" button to book a consultation time and get advice about the suitable treatment.

Tips to get dental photos at home.
2
Treatment Plan & Scheduling
After the consultation, you will get several variants of treatment plans for different budgets. When you will choose a treatment plan, contact us to agree on the trip date and get all necessary recommendations.
3
Flight & Transfer
Book a two-way ticket to the Istanbul Airport (IST or SAW) by yourself at least two weeks in advance, and tell us the booking dates. We will book a hotel for you in the vicinity of the clinic and arrange the arrival/departure airport transfer.
4
Accommodation
You will have a room at a 4-star or 3-star hotel. Your first visit to the clinic will take place the next day after your arrival, so you will have time to relax after the flight. If you want to bring guests with you, please let us know in advance, and we will book suitable rooms and include them in your bill.
5
First Consultation at the Clinic
Our dentists will examine you and make an X-ray, then they will revise and discuss the treatment plan with you one more time. After that we can sign a medical agreement and start the treatment with your consent.
6
Payment
You have to pay for your treatment before it starts. If your treatment requires several visits, then you pay in instalments. It is best to pay in cash. If you want to pay by card, the cost will be increased by 10% + a 3% bank commission.
7
Treatment
The treatment process proceeds according to the steps described above. During the treatment, you will receive all the necessary medicines and recommendations from the dentist.
8
Meals During Treatment
If your treatment involves a special diet, then we can arrange the delivery of food to your hotel (at an additional charge). Or you can eat at restaurants following the dentist's dietary recommendations.
9
Warranty
5 years warranty on all dental work such as implants, veneers or crowns.

The warranty is valid under condition that you:

- Perform the general oral hygiene
- Follow the post-treatment instructions from the dentist
- Have a routine dental check-up and professional cleaning at least once a year
