About Us

DentistryScanner is a UK-based company helping dental tourists find the most reputable and qualified dentists in Turkey at affordable prices.

Through our network of pre-vetted dental practitioners, clients can find dentists who put the interests of the patient first and select the treatment based on the patient's needs and not with the goal of maximizing their own profits.

We apply a Zero-Mistake Policy to our dentists. This means that if any dentist receives three major and verifiable complaints, they are dismissed from our dental network.

We track each dental case from start to finish to ensure its successful completion.
